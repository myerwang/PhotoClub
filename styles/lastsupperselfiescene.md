Show {argument name="central figure" default="Jesus"} seated at the center front of a long rustic wooden table, wearing dark earth-toned biblical robes, extending one arm toward the camera and holding a modern black smartphone to take a group selfie. Include rustic pottery, clay cups, wooden bowls, torn flatbread, and simple food on the table. The background should feature rough plastered stone walls, hanging cloth drapes, wooden pegs, and sparse historical decor. Frame it as a medium-wide group portrait from the phone camera’s point of view, with shallow depth of field, cinematic realism, detailed fabric textures, natural skin tones, and a rich warm brown color palette.
